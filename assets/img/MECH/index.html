About the Conference

International Conference of Advanced Materials & Technologies for Industry 4.0 (ICAMT 4.0) organized by Bannari Amman Institute of Technology during 23-24 March 2023. The objective of the ICAMT 4.0 is to bridge the gap between current industrial demand and innovations in materials and advanced technologies. The key factor of the industry 4.0 is to speed up the manufacturing process efficiently and do the customer favor through the automation and optimization to improve the business sectors. Advancement in materials and technologies such as robotics, additive manufacturing, artificial intelligence and big data analytics, etc are shifting the landscape of the manufacturing industry. It will create a competent prospect for discussion of major issues related to advanced research in materials for their economic exploitation, long term conservation, new development and imaginative uses. It also envisages global networking to inspire innovative research to provide solutions and drive systematic change. Further, eminent scientists across the globe will share their research and future scope for betterment of mankind. In addition, emerging trends of new technologies, Machine tools, Cutting Tools, Manufacturing systems, and simulation tools for solving industrial problems at various levels from the material design to complex manufacturing system. Sessions of the conference will explore innovative as well as revered technologies, research, development and optimization, workforce efficiency and productivity. The ICAMT 4.0 brings leading researches from academia, R&D organization and industries from various region of the world under one roof. This also helps to identify the current demand and emerging area of the basic and applied materials research.

Publications

All the accepted papers will be published in Conference Proceedings. Selected papers meeting the scope, relevance, and novelty will be invited by the Expert Committee to submit in the following SCI and/or Web of Science, Scopus indexed journals as per the publisher norms.

SAJE – Journal of Reinforced Plastics and Composites (SCI Impact factor: 3.710): The contents of the selected papers must fit the aims and scope of the journal. The mandatory criterion for the selected papers submitted for special issue publication is that they must be handled by the Deputy Editor of the journal and peer-reviewed by independent referees in the same way as papers submitted for regular issues of the journal. There is NO publication charge for submitting and publication in the special issue.

Environmental Science and Pollution Research (Scopus & Web of Science Indexed. SCI Impact factor: 5.190): Selected papers from the ICAMT 4.0 related to “Impact of Industry 4.0 on Environmental Sustainability” will be invited for the submission as per the journal requirements.

Archives in Metallurgy and Materials - (Scopus & Web of Science Indexed. SCI Impact factor: 0.663): Original Scientific research articles and review papers in the field of metallurgy and materials sciences will be invited for the online submission with additional publication cost.

Scientific.net - Solid State Phenomena/Key Engineering Materials (Scopus Indexed): Selected papers will be processed in Trans Tech Publishers with additional cost. The authors are invited for the online submission to complete the peer review process.

AIP Conference Proceedings (Web of Science & Scopus Indexed): Suitable papers permissible plagiarism level will be published with additional cost as per journal norms.
